Privacy Policy

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for payment processing and analytics. These providers are obligated to safeguard your data.

5. Your Rights

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
